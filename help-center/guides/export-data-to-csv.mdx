## Export Guide

Export Datafiniti results as a CSV for use in spreadsheets.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run a Search">
    ````bash
    curl -X POST https://api.datafiniti.co/v4/business/search \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -d '{"query": "category:restaurant", "format": "csv"}' > results.csv
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Your CSV">
    Open `results.csv` in Excel or Google Sheets to review the data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
